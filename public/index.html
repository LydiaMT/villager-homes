Villager Home Selector

Choose your Animal Crossing island villagers based on their home exteriors! Use the dropdown menu to filter by house properties like architecture, color, or material.


I created the house data myself. I made up the architecture types, and all of the properties I set are a best guess. If you have feedback on the values I assigned to the homes you can find me on Twitter, Instagram, or GitHub by clicking the social icons below.
